# Market Gap

Current products typically excel in one or two areas but rarely combine them into a single experience.

Examples:

- Great note-taking but weak AI
- Great AI but limited visualization
- Strong graph but poor usability

There is an opportunity to integrate AI-powered search, knowledge visualization, and intuitive note management into one platform.

---

# Cognitive OS Positioning

> Cognitive OS is an AI-powered Second Brain that combines semantic search, interactive knowledge graphs, and intelligent note organization to help students and researchers retrieve knowledge based on meaning rather than exact keywords.

---

# Competitive Advantages

- AI-powered Semantic Search
- Interactive Knowledge Graph
- Centralized Dashboard
- Context-based Information Retrieval
- Student & Researcher Focus

# SWOT Analysis

## Strengths

- AI-powered search
- Knowledge graph
- Modern architecture
- User-focused design

### Weaknesses

- Early-stage product
- No collaboration features
- Limited integrations

### Opportunities

- Growing adoption of AI productivity tools
- Increasing demand for digital learning
- Expansion into research and education markets

### Threats

- Established products like Notion and Obsidian
- Rapid AI innovation
- User switching costs

---

# Key Product Decisions

Based on the competitive analysis, the MVP focuses on:

- AI Semantic Search
- Knowledge Graph
- Fast Note Retrieval
- Simple User Experience

while postponing:

- Team Collaboration
- Mobile Apps
- Third-party Integrations

These features can be introduced in future releases after validating the core product.
