# 👤 User Personas — Cognitive OS

Understanding the primary users helps ensure product decisions are driven by real user needs rather than assumptions.

---

# Primary Persona

## Aarav Sharma

**Age:** 20

**Occupation:** Computer Science Student

**Education:** B.Tech Undergraduate

### Background

Aarav attends multiple lectures daily and studies from YouTube, PDFs, research articles, and handwritten notes. His information is scattered across different platforms, making it difficult to retrieve during assignments and exams.

---

### Goals

- Organize study material in one place
- Quickly find notes during exams
- Connect related concepts across subjects
- Spend less time searching and more time learning

---

### Pain Points

- Notes scattered across multiple apps
- Cannot remember exact keywords
- Difficult to revisit old topics
- Loses track of related ideas

---

### Needs

- AI-powered search
- Organized notes
- Knowledge visualization
- Fast note retrieval

---

### User Story

> As a student, I want to search notes using concepts instead of exact keywords so that I can quickly find information during exams.

---

### How Cognitive OS Helps

- Semantic Search
- Tags
- Dashboard
- Knowledge Graph

---

# Secondary Persona

## Dr. Priya Verma

**Age:** 28

**Occupation:** Research Scholar

### Background

Priya manages hundreds of research papers, observations, and references. She often discovers useful information but struggles to reconnect related ideas over long research projects.

---

### Goals

- Organize research efficiently
- Discover relationships between concepts
- Retrieve papers quickly
- Build a connected knowledge base

---

### Pain Points

- Large volume of notes
- Difficult to discover hidden relationships
- Traditional folders become cluttered
- Keyword search misses contextual information

---

### Needs

- Context-aware search
- Knowledge graph
- Smart organization
- Scalable note management

---

### User Story

> As a researcher, I want related concepts to be automatically connected so I can identify patterns without manually searching through hundreds of notes.

---

### How Cognitive OS Helps

- Semantic Search
- Interactive Knowledge Graph
- Tags
- Centralized Dashboard

---

| Attribute | Student | Researcher |

# Key Product Takeaways

From these personas, the highest priority product capabilities are:

- AI-powered Semantic Search
- Knowledge Graph Visualization
- Centralized Dashboard
- Intelligent Note Organization
- Fast Information Retrieval
